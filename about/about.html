Leveling up

Your main objective is to level up, there are a lot of things to do to level up. The most recommended things to do is killing monsters.

Inventory

To store weapons and items, we provide inventory, you can access it anywhere in the game and access your weapons and items.

Weapons

To kill monsters, you will need weapon. In Jenshin IMpekZ, there are a lot of weapons such as sword, bows, and spear. You can buy weapons from sword or achieve it from mission

Missions

We also gives you a way to leveling up and gain money by doing missions. There are types of mission. First of all daily mission, main mission and side mission. Succeded doing mission will give you rewards, weapons, exp, and gold depends on the mission difficulty

Gold

Here we have our own game currency to buy in the store, We called it GOLD. By using gold you can buy item or weapons in the store. You can get gold by killing monsters or doing mission.

Store

To buy items and weapons, we provide you store. You can access store everywhere. Store provide you a lot of weapons and items you can buy by using gold. Please make sure you have enough gold to buy from the store

Items

Besides weapons, we have items in our game. Items are handy in any situation, you can use it to upgrade your weapon, your character and it can help you to kill monsters. You can get items from missions, killing monster or buy it from stores
